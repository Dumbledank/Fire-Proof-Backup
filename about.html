WHO is Joel Firebrace?

Hello, and welcome to my website portfolio. My name is Joel Firebrace and I’m a concept developer and digital designer.

Background

Over the years since graduating with a degree in physics from the University of Manchester, I have slowly come to realise that I possess a particular interest in the field of creative design.

And after six months in Costa Rica, exposed to the nations captivating natural splendour and biodiversity, ignited within me was both an appreciation and an enthusiasm for the natural world. This affection has since led me to discover an interest in the intersection between ecological conservation and innovative design.

WHAT am I passionate about and WHY?

I am passionate about ideas which purposefully incorporate nature into the built environment.

Why? Because fundamentally, we are a part of nature and depend on it to survive.

It’s surely our responsibility to do everything within our power to create a planet that provides a home not just for us but for all life on earth.- David Attenborough on Cities That Are Saving The Planet

Trees are our closest relatives. What they exhale we inhale, what we exhale, they inhale.- Sadguru on Trees closest relative

WHAT is the intention behind this website?

The intention of this website is to communicate ideas which have captured my interest. Some of these ideas might appear somewhat abstract and artistic in value, while others an attempt to innovate or invent something new and entirely different.

WHAT am I currently working on/ working towards?

My present occupation is developing this website. I am also collating projects I have worked on previously to display in the gallery. In addition to this, I am looking to refine ideas which relate to garden infrastructure such as compost bins, raised beds, living walls, greenhouses and composting toilets.

HOW can I help you?

I can add value to your idea by employing a lateral, out of the box, thinking approach.

And, I can develop a digital prototype of your idea.
